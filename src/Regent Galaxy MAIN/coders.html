The galaxy coders - dream team

The galaxy coders were founded in 2021 by Mr. Troy ellison (head of computing). He had one goal : to make a team of Coders, who were ready to take on any challage. The team, lead by Mr. Ellison, Aseem bagul and sasha bagul, has grown to 12 teammates in just one month. The galaxy coders are found doing extra tasks, like building websites for side teachers like Miss. Walkinshaw (head of music) and building websites of upcoming school events. The team always strive to push thier limits. The motto for the coders is "HardCoders dare to code".
